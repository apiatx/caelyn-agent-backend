Suggested logic:
- breakout favors positive price action, supportive candles, positive trade flow, positive book imbalance, acceptable liquidity
- mean reversion favors stretched mark/oracle or premium dislocation, extreme funding, stretched move
- trend continuation favors sustained move + healthy liquidity + supportive flow
- crowding unwind favors extreme funding + elevated OI + adverse dislocation
- avoid favors wide spreads, weak liquidity, unstable prints, thin books
